Collecting Crystals!








You will be given a random "number to guess" at the start of the game.

Behold these four lovely crystals.Depending on which one of these four crystals you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The only way to win in this game by matching your total score to the "number to guess". If you don't match the score given you lose the game.

The value of each crystal will be hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the set points per crystal will also change!
